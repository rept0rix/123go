Arena AI — Real‑Estate Discovery to Live MVP

Arena AI is a real‑estate intelligence platform helping buyers and agents connect through a conversational discovery journey. The goal was to transform fragmented property data into a structured, chat‑driven experience that feels human and responsive.

Process

  • Problem → Users struggled to explore relevant properties quickly due to inconsistent metadata across sources.
  • Approach → Adaptive conversational layer translating natural input into structured AEO search queries.
  • Design → Modular dashboard with progressive filters and semantic highlights.
  • Outcome → Production MVP enabling property matching and agent handoff in under two months.
Live Demo

Problem & Constraints

Real‑estate sites often overwhelm users with listings but no guidance. Arena reimagines discovery by making the system ask the right questions — “Where do you want to wake up?” instead of endless filters.

Approach & Reasoning

I structured the flow as a chat‑based funnel backed by an AEO JSON layer connecting intents to structured data. Wireframes evolved through three usability rounds.

What I’d Do Next

Add agent‑side analytics and context‑aware recommendations to shorten negotiation cycles.

BuzzonChat — AI‑Driven Checkout & Subscription Logic

BuzzonChat automates GSO for Shopify merchants. The challenge was a multi‑tier checkout and subscription experience that balances transparency, flexibility, and simplicity.

Process

  • Problem → Merchants were confused by complex usage tiers and unpredictable billing.
  • Approach → Visual plan cards tied to product count and usage tokens.
  • Design → Three clear steps with contextual tooltips and inline validation.
  • Outcome → Reduced friction and prepared the foundation for automated onboarding.
Live Site

Problem & Constraints

SaaS billing complexity was causing hesitation. The goal was to craft pricing and checkout that build trust and reduce cognitive load.

Approach & Reasoning

Mapped journeys from free scan to paid upgrade, with edge cases like failed renewals, proration, and credits. Prototypes tested with 6 merchants via Loom walkthroughs.

What I’d Do Next

AI‑guided plan recommender and a progress dashboard that visualizes GSO improvements over time.

FriendLine — Emotional AI for Seniors (Feedback‑Led)

Voice companion connecting seniors with family through weekly AI‑assisted calls. The focus: designing empathy into automation — clarity, warmth, and safety in every interaction.

Process

  • Problem → Seniors often feel isolated and overwhelmed by complex tech interfaces.
  • Approach → Voice‑first flows based on caregiver interviews and emotional mapping.
  • Design → Calm visuals with audio cues, daily summaries, and privacy‑first consent flows.
  • Outcome → Increased engagement and family involvement in care routines.
Live App

Problem & Constraints

Elder users felt technology was talking at them, not with them. FriendLine humanizes automation by giving the interface emotional texture.

Approach & Reasoning

Combined qualitative research (15 caregiver interviews) with sprints focused on tone calibration. Every microcopy validated for empathy and simplicity.

What I’d Do Next

Multilingual voices and mood tracking through conversational cues.
